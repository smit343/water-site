About Us

Serving communities with pride, purpose, and precision in water treatment and infrastructure support.

Who We Are

Water Refined Operating Services (WROS) is a progressive public utility provider specializing in advanced water and wastewater treatment operations. Founded in 2023, WROS has grown into a trusted authority in municipal and industrial water systems across the region.

With a team of experienced engineers, certified plant operators, and sustainability experts, we manage the entire lifecycle of water — from source intake and treatment, to reuse, delivery, and environmental discharge. Our operations serve over 2.5 million residents, hundreds of businesses, and numerous local governments.

At WROS, we combine industry-leading technology with time-tested methods to ensure clean, safe, and sustainable water solutions. Our facilities are optimized for energy efficiency, automation, and rigorous quality monitoring.

What We Do

  • Wastewater Treatment: Managing large-scale treatment facilities using biological, chemical, and membrane filtration technologies.
  • Infrastructure Management: Maintaining thousands of miles of pipelines, pump stations, reservoirs, and metering systems.
  • Water Quality Assurance: Performing over 100,000 water quality tests annually to meet or exceed regulatory standards.
  • Consulting & Project Delivery: Partnering with municipalities and private sectors for sustainable infrastructure development.
  • Emergency Response: Offering rapid deployment teams for water main breaks, contamination events, and service interruptions.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to deliver refined, reliable, and resilient water services through environmental stewardship, technological innovation, and a commitment to public health.

We envision a future where every community has access to safe water infrastructure that supports growth, protects ecosystems, and improves quality of life. At WROS, water isn’t just a service — it’s a promise.
